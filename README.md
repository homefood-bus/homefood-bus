# HomeFood

**HomeFood** is a family-owned food business website designed to promote fresh, homemade meals, catering services, and weekly meal prep for local customers.

The goal of this project is to create a warm, professional, and user-friendly online presence for a family food business. The website helps customers learn about the business, explore available services, view menu items, request catering, and contact the team for orders or inquiries.

## Business Idea

HomeFood is built around the idea of bringing homemade comfort food to the local community. The business is operated by a family with talented cooks who prepare fresh, delicious, and affordable meals using family-style recipes and quality ingredients.

The brand focuses on trust, convenience, and the feeling of home-cooked food. It is designed for customers who want tasty meals without spending time cooking, as well as people who need reliable food service for small events, office lunches, and family gatherings.

## Services

HomeFood offers several food services, including:

- Daily homemade meals
- Weekly meal prep
- Small event catering
- Office lunch catering
- Family-size meal trays
- Custom food orders
- Pickup and local delivery

## Target Customers

This business is designed for:

- Busy families
- Working professionals
- Students
- Office teams
- Local event organizers
- People hosting birthdays, gatherings, or community events
- Customers who prefer fresh, homemade-style food

## Project Goal

The main goal of this project is to build a professional and trustworthy website that helps HomeFood attract local customers, receive online orders, promote catering services, and grow as a family-owned food brand.
